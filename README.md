[FADE IN]

EXT. KOYAMA UNIVERSITY - DAWN

Wide establishing shot. A sprawling Japanese university campus at sunrise. Cherry blossom trees line every walkway. Pink petals drift through golden morning light. Peaceful. Idyllic.

MIREI (V.O.) (In reverent, almost religious whisper) Every era produces one whom fate sets apart. A being of such transcendent beauty that the world itself rearranges around them.

Slow zoom toward a dormitory building. Windows reflect the sunrise.

MIREI (V.O.) This year, that being is first-year literature student... Ren Kaido.

CUT TO:

INT. DORM HALLWAY - CONTINUOUS

Narrow hallway. Students press against windows, phones raised. Muffled screaming.

STUDENT 1 (whisper-shouting) He's coming out! He's coming OUT!

STUDENT 2 I'm not ready! I didn't do my skincare!

STUDENT 3 (crying) I just want him to see me. Even once.

EXT. DORM ENTRANCE - MOMENTS LATER

The door slides open. Silence falls.

REN KAIDO steps out. 19 years old. Lean build. Jet-black hair, slightly messy, falling over one eye. Deep indigo eyes that catch the light. He's wearing a simple white t-shirt, gray hoodie, black jeans. Nothing special. He could be on a magazine cover.

The crowd gasps. A girl drops her phone. A guy walks into a pole.

Ren squints at the sun. He looks tired.

REN (internal, deadpan) Day one. New city. New university. New chance to be normal. Let's see how long that lasts.

A pigeon lands on his shoulder. He absentmindedly pets it. Three people faint.

REN (internal) ...Four seconds. New record.

CUT TO:

INT. HALLWAY - DAY

MIREI SHIROGANE stands on a bench, megaphone in hand. Silver hair flows behind her. She looks like a military commander surveying a battlefield.

MIREI (into megaphone) LISTEN WELL! The Prince's route from Building 7 to the Administration Office will be active from 08:30 to 08:45! Students enrolled in Literature 101 may approach at a distance of no less than five meters! All others must reroute through the east corridor!

Behind her: CHIKA TANIMOTO, holding a clipboard, nodding seriously. Next to her: RUI AKAGAWA, silent, reading a thick book, not looking up.

CHIKA (whispering) Mirei-senpai, the east corridor has a puddle. Someone might slip.

MIREI (not lowering megaphone) Then they will fall with honor.

[CUT TO BLACK]

TITLE CARD: カレシの呪い — "The Curse of Irresistible"

ACT ONE (4:15 - 10:00)
SCENE 1 — DORM ROOM 204
INT. DORM ROOM 204 - DAY

A cramped but cozy dorm room. Two beds, two desks, a window overlooking campus. Boxes still half-unpacked.

Ren stands in the doorway. His key is in his hand.

On his bed: A stranger. Male. Messy brown hair. Lazy half-closed eyes. He's eating Ren's cup ramen with one hand and playing a handheld console with the other.

SORA (without looking up) Yo. You Ren? Cool. Got any hot water? This ramen's good but it's cold now.

Ren stares.

REN ...That's my ramen.

SORA (glancing up, flashing a grin) Was. I'm Sora. Sora Takamura. I was supposed to be in 206, but 206 smells like feet. So we're roommates now.

Sora goes back to gaming. Slurps ramen.

REN I didn't agree to that.

SORA You will. I'm very persuasive.

Sora points vaguely at a pile of snacks on the other desk.

SORA Also I have snacks. See? I come bearing offerings. That's how roommate partnerships work. It's in the constitution.

Ren opens his mouth. Closes it. For the first time in years, he realizes: this stranger isn't staring at his face. Hasn't blinked twice. Doesn't care.

REN (internal, soft) ...Huh.

REN (aloud) You're staring at your game.

SORA Yeah. It's got good graphics. Why, you want me to stare at you instead?

REN (slight, almost invisible smile) No. Don't.

SORA Good, 'cause I won't. You're just some guy. I've got a raid in ten minutes. You need anything, don't ask me. I'm useless.

Ren sets down his bag. He sits on the edge of his bed. He looks at Sora. Sora continues gaming, oblivious.

REN (internal) ...Maybe this place is different.

SCENE 2 — CAMPUS WALKWAY
EXT. CAMPUS - LATER

Ren walks across campus. Students part around him like water around a stone. Whispers follow.

He takes out his phone. Checks a map. Frowns.

REN (mutters) Literature building is... that way? No, that's the science wing. Maybe...?

He takes a wrong turn.

SCENE 3 — MECHATRONICS LAB
INT. ENGINEERING BUILDING - MECHATRONICS LAB

Heavy door. Sign: "MECHATRONICS LAB — AUTHORIZED ONLY."

Ren pushes it open by accident, distracted by his phone.

Inside: A large workshop. Workbenches covered in wires, circuit boards, half-built machines. Sparks fly in the corner.

At the center workbench: YUNA HOSHINO. 19. Small build. Dark blue ponytail. Safety goggles. She's welding a four-legged metal spider. Her focus is absolute.

YUNA (muttering angrily at the robot) — and THIS leg angle is STUPID. Who designed this? Oh wait. I designed this. Great. I'm an idiot. Perfect.

Ren steps closer, fascinated. The robot twitches. Its legs move unevenly.

REN (quiet, observing) The servo joint on the third leg is misaligned. It's lagging by about 12 degrees.

Yuna's goggles snap up. She whirls.

YUNA Who the hell are —

She sees him.

Off-beat silence.

YUNA (slowly) ...Oh. You're that guy. The pretty one everyone won't shut up about.

REN (blinking) ...Pretty?

YUNA (pointing her welding torch at him, accusatory) I don't know what you did to my robot, but it's angry now. The energy readings spiked the second you walked in. Are you carrying some kind of magnetic generator?

REN No, I'm carrying... a wallet. A phone. Crippling social anxiety. Standard stuff.

YUNA (squinting at him, unimpressed) You're weird. Leave.

She turns back to the robot. Pauses. Glances over her shoulder.

YUNA ...What did you say about the servo?

Ren points. She follows his gesture. Examines the joint. Her eyes narrow.

YUNA (muttering) ...Damn it. He's right.

She adjusts something. The leg moves smoothly.

YUNA ...Tch. Beginner's luck. Don't come back.

REN (small, genuine smile — he can't help it) Wouldn't dream of it.

He walks out.

The door closes. Yuna stands still. Her hand hovers over her chest.

YUNA (internal, confused) ...Why is my heart beating faster? That's not... I haven't even had coffee yet.

She shakes her head violently.

YUNA (aloud) SHUT UP, HEART.

SCENE 4 — THE OBSESSION CLUB MEETING
INT. CLUBROOM - EVENING

A dimly lit room. Candles. A whiteboard covered in photos, red strings, and handwritten notes.

Mirei stands at the board. Chika sits cross-legged on the floor, taking notes. Rui reads in the corner, silent.

MIREI (pointing at a photo of Ren buying a drink from a vending machine) Observation 47: The Prince purchased a green tea at 3:14 PM today. This is 47 minutes earlier than yesterday's tea acquisition. He also made eye contact with the vending machine for 2.3 seconds longer than usual.

CHIKA (writing furiously) 2.3 seconds... noted... what does it mean?!

MIREI (grave) I don't know yet. But we will find out.

RUI (without looking up from her book) (under her breath) You're all obsessed with a walking spiritual disaster.

MIREI What was that?

RUI (silent. turns page.)

[ACT ONE END]

ACT TWO (10:00 - 16:30)
SCENE 5 — NIGHT WALK
EXT. CAMPUS - NIGHT

Ren can't sleep. He walks the empty campus in his hoodie. Streetlights cast long shadows. It's quiet. Peaceful.

REN (internal) First day done. Met a weird roommate. A scary engineer girl. Three love letters slipped under my door. Standard.

He stops. A narrow path between old trees. Overgrown. Mossy.

REN (internal) Didn't see this on the campus map.

He should turn back. He steps forward instead.

SCENE 6 — THE HIDDEN SHRINE
EXT. FORGOTTEN SHRINE - NIGHT

The path opens to a small clearing. Ancient trees circle a stone shrine covered in moss and vines. A small torii gate, weathered, tilting.

The air is heavy. Still. Wrong.

REN (whisper) ...What is this place?

He steps closer. The ground vibrates faintly under his feet. The shrine begins to glow — a soft, pulsing blue-green light.

Ren stops. He should run.

He reaches out instead.

VOICE (old, female, echoing, DIRECTLY IN HIS MIND) You return.

Ren freezes.

VOICE Blood of my keeper after six hundred years. The seal weakens. The one below stirs in its sleep.

The shrine pulses. The ground trembles harder.

VOICE Find the girl of amber eyes and iron will. She is the key. You are the lock. And if you break... if you let her go... if you choose wrong...

A low, guttural LAUGH — deep, male, ancient — rises from beneath the earth. The shrine's glow flickers red.

VOICE He rises.

Ren stumbles back. Falls. Hits the ground hard.

A flashlight clicks on, shining directly into his face.

PRINCIPAL KIRISHIMA (cheerful) Lovely evening for a walk, Kaido-kun!

Ren screams.

[CUT TO: REN SCREAMING, THEN SUDDEN SILENCE]

SCENE 7 — PRINCIPAL'S GAME
EXT. FORGOTTEN SHRINE - CONTINUOUS

Principal Kirishima stands over Ren, flashlight under his chin like a campfire ghost story. Bald head. Round glasses. Oversized tan suit. Smiling.

REN (gasping) Principal?! What are you —

KIRISHIMA That's a wonderful question! The answer is complicated. But here's a simpler one!

He crouches down, eye level with Ren. The smile softens. For a moment, he looks serious.

KIRISHIMA (quiet) Do you believe in love, Kaido-kun?

Ren stares.

REN ...Excuse me?

KIRISHIMA (brightening again) Think about it! Your answer will determine many things! But not now. Now you should sleep. Freshmen need their rest!

Kirishima stands. Turns. Walks back into the darkness, humming a folk song.

REN (shouting after him) That's not an explanation!

KIRISHIMA (voice fading) Nothing ever is, Kaido-kun! Goodnight!

Silence.

Ren sits alone in the dark. The shrine has stopped glowing. The air is still again.

He slowly gets up. Brushes leaves off his hoodie. Looks at the shrine one more time. It's just a pile of old stones now.

REN (whisper) ...What kind of college did I enroll in?

SCENE 8 — SORA'S MYSTERY
INT. DORM ROOM 204 - NIGHT

Ren returns. He opens the door.

Sora is passed out on the floor. Handheld console in one hand. Face against the carpet. Drooling.

Ren steps over him. Sits on his bed.

He looks at his own reflection in the dark window.

REN (internal) A curse. A shrine. A voice in my head. A principal who speaks in riddles. And that girl — the engineer. Yuna. "Amber eyes and iron will."

He closes his eyes.

The camera slowly zooms into his reflection's face.

Ren's real eyes: closed.

His reflection's eyes: OPEN.

The reflection SMILES. Wrong. Too wide.

REFLECTION (voice: ancient, layered, wrong) Everything, little keeper.

Ren's eyes snap open. He whirls around.

The room is empty. Just Sora, asleep, snoring.

Ren looks back at the window. His reflection is normal again. Looking back at him, terrified.

[ACT TWO END]

ACT THREE (16:30 - 21:00)
SCENE 9 — THE NEXT MORNING
INT. DORM ROOM 204 - MORNING

Sunlight streams through the window. Ren is already awake, sitting on his bed, staring at his hands. Dark circles under his eyes.

Sora stirs on the floor.

SORA (groggy) ...Nnngh. Why am I on the floor.

REN You fell asleep there.

SORA Did I beat the boss?

REN I don't know what that means.

SORA (sitting up, stretching) It means did I win. That's all that matters in life.

Sora stands, cracks his neck, shuffles toward the door.

SORA I'm getting breakfast. Want some? I'm not sharing, but I'll let you watch me eat.

REN ...Thanks. I'll pass.

Sora shrugs. He's about to leave when he pauses. Looks back at Ren.

SORA (unusually serious) Yo. You okay? You look like you saw a ghost.

Ren hesitates.

REN ...Just a weird first day.

Sora studies him for a moment. Then grins.

SORA Yeah, well, get used to it. College is just one long weird day after another. That's the secret.

He leaves.

Ren exhales. Stands. Resolves himself.

REN (aloud, to himself) Okay. Fine. Something's happening. I don't know what. But I'm not going to figure it out sitting here.

He grabs his bag. Heads out.

SCENE 10 — CAMPUS ENCOUNTER (REN AND YUNA)
EXT. CAMPUS WALKWAY - DAY

Ren walks with purpose. Students still stare, still whisper, but he barely notices.

He sees Yuna ahead. She's carrying a heavy-looking metal crate, struggling.

REN (internal) "Amber eyes and iron will." The voice said I need to find her.

He approaches.

REN (aloud) Need help with that?

Yuna glares at him.

YUNA No.

She stumbles. The crate nearly falls. She catches it, but barely.

REN ...You sure?

YUNA (straining) I don't need help from a guy who breaks robots by looking at them.

REN I didn't break it. I fixed it.

Yuna stops. Sets the crate down. Turns to face him fully.

YUNA Why do you keep showing up?

REN I don't know. I think the universe is trying to tell me something.

They stand in silence. Cherry blossoms drift between them.

YUNA (quiet, searching his face) ...You're weird.

REN You've mentioned.

YUNA And you're too pretty. It's distracting.

REN ...I've been told.

YUNA (sighs, picks up the crate) Fine. You can carry this. But only because my arms are about to fall off.

Ren takes the crate. It's heavier than it looks. He grunts.

REN What's in this? Bricks?

YUNA (walking ahead, small smile she hides) Robot parts. Try to keep up, pretty boy.

Ren follows. For the first time in the episode, he smiles — real, warm.

REN (internal) Amber eyes and iron will. Yeah... I think I found her.

SCENE 11 — THE WATCHER
EXT. CAMPUS - DISTANT BALCONY

Wide shot of the campus. A figure stands on a distant balcony, watching Ren and Yuna walk away together.

The figure is female. Long black hair. Pale skin. Dark kimono. She holds a broken hand mirror.

FIGURE (cold, soft) The keeper and the priestess. Already walking side by side.

She looks at her mirror. The reflection shows Ren and Yuna — but distorted. Shadows cling to them. Chains wrap around their wrists, connecting them.

FIGURE How precious. How fragile.

She smiles. It's not a kind smile.

FIGURE Let's see how long they stay together.

She raises her hand. Snaps her fingers.

[SOUND: DISTANT BELL TOLLING]

CLIMAX (21:00 - 22:15)
SCENE 12 — THE SEAL WEAKENS
INT. DORM ROOM 204 - NIGHT (LATER)

Ren lies in bed. Staring at the ceiling.

Sora is on his bed, gaming.

SORA You're being quiet. Like, quieter than usual. Did something happen?

REN ...Do you believe in curses?

Sora pauses his game. Looks at Ren.

SORA (slowly) That's a weird question.

REN I know.

SORA ...I think some things are cursed. Some things are blessed. Most things are just... things. People give them meaning.

Ren looks at him.

REN That's surprisingly deep for you.

SORA (grinning, unpausing his game) I have my moments. Now shut up. I'm about to beat this level.

Ren smiles. Closes his eyes.

[SUBTLE EFFECT: The shrine glows in the dark of campus. A crack forms on its surface.]

[SUBTLE SOUND: A heartbeat. Deep. Rhythmic. Coming from underground.]

Ren's eyes snap open. He looks at the window.

The moon is red.

REN (whisper) ...Something's wrong.
